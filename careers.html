Our Values

Uplevel

We do not allow the constraints of what is given today to dictate what greatness could look like tomorrow.

We believe diverse perspectives from our peers strengthens our work, our minds, and our mission. Collaboration, iteration, and two-way feedback are celebrated.

Uplift

We are consumer-first. Transparency and empowerment guide us, with every decision reflecting, “How would our customer feel?”

We invite bold ideas from every team member, and reward thoughtfulness to collectively create the best solutions. We challenge each other with purpose and openness to create meaningful change.

Uphold

We align on the best possible decision through rigorous discussion and testing. United as a team, we set aside personal biases to fully honor and uphold our agreements.

We ensure the customer has the best product on the market, the best support, and the most seamless experience. Our duty is to deliver excellence.
