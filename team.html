Our Vision

“Today, we don’t have the capacity to engage individually with all our customers...leaving a lot of opportunity on the table”

— Customer Success, Zendesk

”Our time should be spent talking to customers, not digging through data to understand what to talk to them about”

— Account Management, Shopify

We get it. Customer-facing teams are often juggling endless accounts. Inevitably, some customers - real people with unique needs - get overlooked. Even with the best intentions, outreach can easily miss the mark. Today's customers' are flooded with content that feels too generic, too irrelevant, or poorly timed, cluttering their mindshare and weakening loyalty. It's hard to get it right. But we think we might just have.

MineMe is taking the guesswork out of outreach, and helping client-facing teams deliver individualized, 1:1 service to every customer - not just their biggest ones. Our algorithms combine the most crucial pieces of a customer's data with real-time public information, predicting what they need next and how to message it. Our AI copilot crafts hyper-personalized outreach and works with you to help every customer feel seen, valued, and understood - regardless of their size or shape.

We're on a mission to make every customer feel like your only customer - but hey, you don't have to do all the work. We handle the heavy lifting, so you can unlock 100% of the opportunity on 100% of your accounts.
